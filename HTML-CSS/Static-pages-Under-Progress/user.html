FAQ

How can I pay my rent?

You can pay your rent on using your credit card or debit card on the first ten days.

How can I request maintenance to my apartment?

You can fill service request from on stating the issue to be fixed and submitting it. Then check the status of the request if it is fixed or not. Else contact the building management office.

How sign up for a gym?

You can apply for gym filling service request form. Then pay the amount for half year or full year. If you want to pay a check submit it to the management office.
